# Home Page

This skill defines the core structure of the landing experience, ensuring immediate professional credibility and clear navigation to Geomatic services.

## Primary Directives

- **High-Impact Visuals**: Use full-width hero imagery of precise instrument setups (Total Stations/Scanners) to establish technical authority.
- **Conversion-Centric**: Every section must lead toward the "Request a Quote" or "Our Services" endpoints.
- **UK Market Alignment**: Use British English standards and reference UK-specific project contexts.

<details>
<summary>Hero Section & Value Proposition (Click to expand)</summary>

- **Headline**: "Precision Surveying & Geomatic Engineering Solutions."
- **Sub-headline**: "Delivering high-accuracy spatial data to de-risk construction and design projects across the UK."
- **Primary CTA**: "Request a Quote" (Scrolls to Contact or links to /contact).
- **Secondary CTA**: "Explore Our Services" (Links to /services).
</details>

<details>
<summary>Component Structure (Click to expand)</summary>

### 1. Featured Services Grid
Display the 6 core services using the **Floating Overlay Card** pattern.
- Refer to `Our Services/SKILL.md` for titles and concepts.
- Interaction: Hover to reveal sub-service lists.

### 2. Industry Sectors Ribbon
A clean, icon-based or text-based scroller highlighting key sectors:
- **Architecture & Planning**, **Infrastructure & Highways**, **Construction & Civil Engineering**, etc.

### 3. "Latest Projects" (Portfolio Preview)
Showcase the top 3 entries from `project.json` where `featured: true`.
- Use the "Floating Overlay" pattern to show site photos and project names.
- Link directly to the full case study or the relevant Service page.

### 4. Trust Signals
- Mention adherence to RICS/ICES standards (if applicable).
- Display a "24-Hour Quotation Turnaround" badge.
</details>
