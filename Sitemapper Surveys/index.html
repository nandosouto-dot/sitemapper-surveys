Who We Are

Engineering-Grade Survey Data,
Delivered on Schedule

Sitemapper Surveys is a UK-based geomatics practice specialising in topographic surveys, measured building surveys, 3D laser scanning, and construction setting-out. We combine GNSS positioning, robotic total stations, and terrestrial LiDAR to deliver BIM-ready datasets — from initial site appraisal through to as-built documentation.

Core Services

What We Deliver

Topographic Surveys

Detailed ground-level surveys using GNSS and total station equipment. Deliverables include .DWG plans, contour maps, and digital terrain models compliant with RICS and OS standards.

3D Scanning & BIM

Terrestrial LiDAR scanning for registered point clouds and Scan-to-BIM modelling. Ideal for refurbishment, heritage, and complex structural projects requiring millimetre-accurate spatial data.

Setting Out

Precision construction setting-out for foundations, steelwork, walls, roads, and infrastructure. We transfer design coordinates to site with sub-5mm accuracy using robotic total stations.
